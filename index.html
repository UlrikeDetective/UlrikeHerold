Hello

Data Whiz with a Creative Soul

By day, I navigate through the councils of the City of Leipzig, making sure their work tell a clear story. But when I'm not wrangling agendas, motions, notes, dashboards and spreadsheets, you can find me...

Wandering through art galleries, biennales, museums and streets and letting the art spark my own creativity. (I love using Lightroom, AI, and Photoshop to capture the world through my lens!)

Or maybe I'm gliding through a river on my (rented) kayak, or exploring new paths on two wheels. Nature is my happy place, and a good dose of yoga keeps me balanced.

So, what's the connection? It's all about finding beauty and understanding. Data tells a story, art tells a story, and nature whispers its own wisdom. I love finding the connections and sharing them with the world!
